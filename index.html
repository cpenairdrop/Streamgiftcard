The Pine: Comfort, Value & Community

Discover the perfect blend of comfort, value, and community with The Pine, a beautifully designed 1,964 sq. ft. single-family home located in the highly sought-after Pinegrove neighborhood of La Plata, Maryland. This spacious two-level home features 4 bedrooms and 2.5 bathrooms, offering the ideal layout for today’s modern family.

Step inside to an open-concept main floor that makes everyday living effortless—whether it’s family dinners, game nights, or entertaining friends. The thoughtfully planned second level includes a private owner’s suite, generous secondary bedrooms, and convenient storage, giving every family member room to grow.

Living in Pinegrove means more than just owning a home—it’s joining a welcoming, family-focused community. Tree-lined streets, peaceful surroundings, and that small-town charm make this neighborhood a place where memories are made and roots are planted.

With La Plata’s affordability, owning a spacious single-family home becomes an attainable path to long-term wealth and stability. Enjoy the comfort of a brand-new home, modern construction, and the value of a community that truly feels like home—all just a short drive from major commuter routes, shopping, dining, and top-rated schools.
